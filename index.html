About Me

I'm a 5th year graduate student and Ph.D. candidate at UC Riverside working with Dr. Laura Sales on computational galaxy formation. This research was a natural outgrowth of my coding and interest in cosmology. I am also very interested in issues related to diversity and inclusivity, both in STEM academia and more broadly, leading to my work with the Physics Organization for Womxn and the Under-Represented (POWUR), of which I am currently vice president.
