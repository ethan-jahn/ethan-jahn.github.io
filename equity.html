Equity

Beyond my research and teaching duties, I am a strong advocate for Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion in my department and the University at large. I am the current president of the Physics Organization for Womxn and the Under-Represented (POWUR), and the founder of our Inclusivity Seminar series. I am also a member of UCR's DEI Climate Council, which advocates for the concrete realization of the values of inclusivity, respect, and openness throughout the university.
